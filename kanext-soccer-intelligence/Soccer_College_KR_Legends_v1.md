## Governance Note
College legends mirror the basketball intelligence architecture exactly. Each level has its own legend with KR tier descriptions anchored to soccer-specific production (goals, assists, minutes, awards, team success). All ratings assume KLVN normalization. College KR reflects current college role and impact only. No transfer projection, no pro draft language.

v1 notes:
1. All draft/pro/MLS projection language excluded. College KR is present-tense only.
2. Production benchmarks are per-season (not per-90) because college stat infrastructure is less standardized than professional.
3. Position-specific benchmarks provided at key tiers for forwards (F), midfielders (M), and defenders (D).
4. College soccer seasons are ~20 matches (conference + nonconference + tournament). Volume benchmarks reflect this.

To read a player's legend: use the player's home level key to select the correct legend file, then find the tier matching their KR.

For Level Tier Map (cross-level reads): read the same KR against multiple legend files to show what the number means at each level. One KR, multiple legend reads.

# NCAA DIVISION I -- HIGH-MAJOR SOCCER PLAYER KR LEGEND v1
## lambda = 1.000

Interpretation anchor: ACC, Big Ten, Big 12, SEC, Big East, Pac-12 (where applicable). Deep rosters, national recruiting, sustained play vs Top-50 RPI opponents. College KR reflects current college role and impact only. No pro language, no MLS projection. All ratings assume KLVN normalization.

**98-100 -- National Player of the Year / Hermann Trophy Contender.**
Program-defining force. Elite goal output AND elite creation simultaneously. Warps opposing game plans. Drives team to College Cup contention. MAC Hermann Trophy finalist or winner. Reserved for generational single-season performers.
- F benchmark: 18+ goals, 8+ assists, 40+ points season, .300+ shot conversion, first-choice on a top-10 RPI side.
- M benchmark: 10+ goals, 12+ assists, All-American first team, drives possession AND scores in tournament play. Dominates both phases.
- D benchmark: Not typical at this tier. Would require an elite ball-playing CB/FB who also anchors the #1 defensive record in the country AND contributes 5+ goals/assists from the back.

**95-97 -- Franchise Anchor / Elite All-American.**
Team's unquestioned alpha. All-American lock. Conference Offensive or Defensive POY contender. Carries the attacking or defensive load nightly. 80+ minutes/match on a team that reaches the NCAA Tournament Sweet 16 or deeper. Cannot be replaced.
- F benchmark: 14-17 goals, 6-8 assists, 34-39 points, starter every match on a top-15 side.
- M benchmark: 8-10 goals, 8-12 assists, All-Conference first team, drives tempo and creation for a tournament-caliber team.
- D benchmark: All-American CB/FB. Team concedes fewest goals in conference. Anchors the defensive identity. 0.60 or fewer GA/90 when on the field.

**92-94 -- High-Impact Starter / Core Winner.**
Wins matches at the highest college level. Can be an offensive alpha or a two-way anchor whose completeness stabilizes everything. Heavy-minute leader. All-Conference first team. Trusted in tournament play. Drives outcomes against elite competition.
- F benchmark: 10-13 goals, 4-6 assists, 24-33 points. Regular starter on a ranked team.
- M benchmark: 5-8 goals, 6-8 assists. Two-way dominance. Controls the midfield in big matches.
- D benchmark: All-Conference defender. Team is top-25 in goals allowed. Dominant in the air and on the ball.

**89-91 -- Solid Starter / Top-Five Rotation Lock.**
Firmly positive starter value at D1 HM. 80+ minutes in most matches. Consistent two-way impact. All-Conference second team or honorable mention range. The starters on ranked teams who aren't the stars but who you can't win without.
- F benchmark: 6-9 goals, 3-5 assists. Reliable contributor. Starts conference play.
- M benchmark: 3-5 goals, 4-6 assists. Does the dirty work in midfield. Wins duels, keeps possession.
- D benchmark: Reliable starter. Clean in 1v1 duels. Rarely beaten. Comfortable on the ball.

**86-88 -- Trusted Rotation / High-Minute Role Player.**
Winning-role player who thrives in a defined role. 60+ minutes in meaningful matches. Value comes from one or more specialties: set piece delivery, defensive heading, wing speed, ball retention, pressing intensity.
- F benchmark: 3-5 goals, 2-4 assists. Impact sub or specialist starter.
- M benchmark: 1-3 goals, 2-4 assists. Energy, pressing, or distribution specialist.
- D benchmark: Dependable. Fills in at multiple positions. Low error rate.

**83-85 -- Reliable Bench / Rotation Contributor.**
True rotation depth on good teams. 40-60 minutes average. Consistent energy or specialty. No major drop-off when on the field. The 12th-14th man on a ranked team.

**80-82 -- Situational Specialist / Depth Piece.**
Matchup- and context-dependent contributor. 20-40 minutes. Role-specific value.

**77-79 -- Limited Bench / Emergency Depth.**
Playable only under constraint. Spot minutes.

**74-76 -- Fringe Roster / Non-Rotation.**
On the roster, not in the competitive plan.

**71-73 -- Developmental / Project.**
Future-oriented roster slot. Physically or skill-wise incomplete. Not currently viable in HM games.

**68-70 -- Practice Roster / Walk-On.**
Roster filler for structure, not competition.

**Below 68 -- Below D1 HM Viability.**
