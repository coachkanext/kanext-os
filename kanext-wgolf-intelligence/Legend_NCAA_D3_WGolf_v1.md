## D3 EVALUATION CONTEXT

**D3 women's golf has NO athletic scholarships.** Players choose D3 for academic programs, campus culture, and the opportunity to compete in college golf without the pressures of scholarship athletics. This means D3 rosters include a wider range of talent levels than scholarship programs.

**D3 field quality varies enormously.** Top D3 programs (Williams, Methodist, NYU, Centre, Carnegie Mellon) field players who would be competitive at D2 and some D1 low-major programs. Weaker D3 programs may have players who are largely recreational.

**D3 scoring averages are typically 4-8 strokes higher than D1 Power 4.** A 76.0 scoring average at D3 is strong. A 74.0 or below is elite for the level.

**D3 National Championship is stroke play (72 holes).** Selecting the correct field and course for context is critical. The NCAA D3 Championship field is the best of D3 and performances there are the strongest evaluation data points.
