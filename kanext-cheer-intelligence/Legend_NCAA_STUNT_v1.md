# NCAA STUNT PLAYER KR LEGEND v1
## Lambda = 1.000 (reference level)

Interpretation anchor: NCAA Division I and II STUNT programs. Baylor, Oregon, Davenport, Adrian, Azusa Pacific, Quinnipiac, Gannon, and other programs in the growing STUNT landscape. Head-to-head match format with quarter-by-quarter skill category testing. Scholarship availability varies by institution (STUNT is an emerging NCAA sport, scholarship allocation is program-specific). Competition is structured, measurable, and growing rapidly. The talent pool is deepening each year as more programs launch.

**92-100 - STUNT All-American / Elite National Standout.**
Dominant force at the NCAA STUNT level. Wins individual matchups at an elite rate regardless of opponent. Defines the team's ability to compete for a national championship. The athlete the program markets, the opponent game-plans around, and the one who makes the biggest plays in the biggest moments. Skills are at or near the ceiling of what the STUNT format tests. All-American lock.

**88-91 - National-Level Impact / Conference POY Contender.**
Top-tier athlete who drives team success at the highest level. Anchor of their stunt group or primary tumbling weapon. Conference award contender. Reliable in every quarter. High matchup win rate against quality opponents. Programs are built around 2-3 athletes at this level.

**84-87 - Franchise Starter / Core Identity.**
Primary contributor who defines the team's competitive identity in at least one quarter. Starts every match. Performs the team's highest-difficulty skills. Conference all-star caliber. The athlete whose absence would significantly change the team's scoring ceiling.

**80-83 - High-Impact Starter / Reliable Anchor.**
Consistent starter who contributes across multiple quarters. Strong skill execution. Low deduction rate. Trusted in pressure situations. Not the flashiest athlete but one of the most dependable. Top 8-10 on any nationally competitive roster.

**76-79 - Solid Starter / Rotation Lock.**
Reliable performer in the starting lineup or first sub. Contributes clean execution in assigned skills. May not perform the highest-difficulty skills but what they do, they do well. Valuable for team depth and quarter coverage.

**72-75 - Rotation Contributor / Specialist.**
In the active competition rotation. Contributes in specific quarters or skill categories. May be the team's 11th-16th athlete. Specialist value (e.g., elite tumbler who only competes in Q2). Wins their role matchups at a decent rate.

**68-71 - Depth / Developmental Starter.**
On the competition roster but in a limited role. May sub in for specific skills or as injury replacement. Practices with the competition squad. Could start within a year with development.

**64-67 - Practice Squad / Near-Roster.**
Participates in full practice but not in the competition rotation. Close to contributing. One skill upgrade or one chemistry development cycle away from competition readiness.

**60-63 - Developmental Athlete.**
On the roster for future potential. Learning the system. Needs multiple skill progressions before competing. Valuable practice partner.

**56-59 - Walk-On / Beginner-Level.**
Roster filler at this level. Minimal competitive pathway at the NCAA STUNT level. May contribute to practice but not to competition outcomes.

**Below 56 - Below NCAA STUNT Viability.**
Below the competitive threshold for NCAA STUNT programs. Needs significant development or a different competitive level.
