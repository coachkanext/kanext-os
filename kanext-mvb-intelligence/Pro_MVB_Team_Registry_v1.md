# Pro Men's Volleyball Team Registry v1

---

## SCOPE

This registry contains the major professional men's volleyball clubs worldwide, organized by league. Used for pro transition placement, salary benchmarking, and league-level intelligence.

**Note:** Professional volleyball rosters change annually. Use web search to verify current rosters and import slots when evaluating specific placement targets.
