# PRO WOMEN'S GOLF KR LEGEND
## v1.0

---

## Purpose
Maps KR ratings to professional women's golf performance tiers. Each tier describes what a player at that KR level looks like on the LPGA Tour (reference level) and equivalent tiers on other women's professional tours. Earnings projections are based on 2025-26 purse structures and historical distributions.

# PREGNANCY/MOTHERHOOD IN PRO GOLF

## Impact on KR and Earnings

When a professional golfer becomes pregnant and takes maternity leave:

**KR Treatment:**
- Pre-pregnancy KR is preserved as the "baseline KR"
- Upon return, current production is evaluated normally, producing a "return KR"
- Both are reported until return KR reaches within 3 points of baseline KR
- Pregnancy/Motherhood Suppression flag is applied to the gap
- Typical return timeline: 6-18 months to competitive golf, 12-24 months to pre-pregnancy production

**Earnings Treatment:**
- Lost earnings are modeled as: Pre-pregnancy annual earnings x (months absent / 12)
- Career earnings projections are adjusted to account for the absence period
- The Motherhood Return Override (+1.0 KR) applies when production returns to baseline within 18 months

**Tour Status Protections:**
- LPGA: Medical extension preserves exempt status for 1 year
- Epson Tour: Similar medical protections
- LET: Maternity provisions in tour regulations
- KLPGA/JLPGA: Vary by tour rules

**Career Longevity Context:**
- Women's golf careers extend longer than most sports (competitive into late 30s and 40s)
- A 2-3 year pregnancy pause in the mid-to-late 20s does not end a career
- Several LPGA players have returned to high-level competition after motherhood
- The system never treats pregnancy suppression as permanent decline

## GOVERNANCE
- Pro KR Legend tiers are descriptive. They do not produce KR.
- Earnings projections are based on historical data and current purse structures. Actual earnings will vary.
- Tour lambdas are calibrated to reflect relative field strength across professional tours.
- All KR values are universal. Reading a KR against this legend provides tour-specific context but does not change the KR itself.
