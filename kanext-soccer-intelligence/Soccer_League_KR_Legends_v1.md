# SOCCER LEAGUE PLAYER KR LEGENDS -- v1 (CONSOLIDATED)

## Purpose
Each league legend provides tier-by-tier descriptions of what a KR means in that competitive environment. Legends are display-only -- they interpret KR values, they do not produce or modify them.

## Lambda Source
All lambda values are from the canonical KLVN table in File 02 (Soccer Player Eval Reference v1). Any discrepancy defaults to File 02.

## Position-Specific Production Benchmarks
Each legend includes striker (ST) and midfielder (CM) benchmark profiles at key KR tiers to anchor what "good" looks like in that league. All stats are per-90 unless noted.

# PREMIER LEAGUE KR LEGEND v1
## lambda = 1.000

Interpretation anchor: The reference league. Deepest squads, highest average competition density, global recruitment. All other leagues calibrate against this. KR reflects current league role and impact only. No transfer projection language.

**98-100 -- Global Apex / Transcendent.**
Program-defining force. One of the best players in world football. Ballon d'Or contender. Game-warps systems and wins titles as the decisive individual. Golden Boot or Playmaker of the Season lock. Reserved for generational single-season performers.
- ST benchmark: 0.85+ G/90, 0.75+ xG/90, 25+ league goals, top-4 side primary scorer, PL Golden Boot contender. (Anchors: Haaland 22/23, Salah 23/24)
- CM benchmark: Not typical. Would require 90+ KR across all four components simultaneously -- generational two-way midfielder. (Anchor: Rodri 23/24 Ballon d'Or)

**94-97 -- Elite Franchise Star.**
All-league first XI. Top-5 positional player in the league. Decisive in big matches, Champions League knockouts. Team identity built around this player. Cannot be replaced without structural rebuild.
- ST benchmark: 0.60-0.84 G/90, 0.55-0.74 xG/90, 15-24 league goals, consistent starter on a top-6 side, regular CL contributor. (Anchors: Saka, Palmer, Son at peak)
- CM benchmark: 8.0+ progressive passes/90, 3.0+ tackles+interceptions/90, 88%+ pass accuracy, 2800+ minutes, PFA Team of the Year contender. (Anchors: Rice, Bruno Fernandes at peak)

**90-93 -- High-Impact Star / Best XI Lock.**
First-choice starter on a top-6 or strong mid-table side. Best XI most weeks. Trusted closer. All-league nomination range. Drives outcomes against quality opposition.
- ST benchmark: 0.45-0.59 G/90, 0.40-0.54 xG/90, 10-16 league goals, regular starter 2500+ minutes, clear first-choice.
- CM benchmark: 6.0-7.9 progressive passes/90, 2.2-2.9 tackles+interceptions/90, 85-87% pass accuracy, 2500+ minutes. Consistent two-way performer.

**86-89 -- Core Starter / Reliable First-Choice.**
Nailed-on starter. Could start for most PL sides. Consistent performer week in, week out. Does not carry the team but is a clear net positive.
- ST benchmark: 0.30-0.44 G/90, 0.28-0.39 xG/90, 7-12 league goals, 2000+ minutes.
- CM benchmark: 4.0-5.9 progressive passes/90, 1.5-2.1 tackles+interceptions/90, 83-85% pass accuracy. Reliable in role.

**82-85 -- Rotation / Squad Contributor.**
Regular rotation player. Starts 15-25 league matches. Trusted in cup and European games. Quality squad depth.
- ST benchmark: 0.20-0.29 G/90, 5-8 league goals, 1200-1999 minutes. Often subbed on/off.
- CM benchmark: Solid in limited minutes. Maintains team shape and tempo without elevating.

**78-81 -- Fringe First-Team / Depth.**
Occasional starter. Reliable depth. Might start for a lower-table PL side. First off the bench.
- ST benchmark: 0.15-0.19 G/90, 3-5 league goals, 800-1200 minutes.
- CM benchmark: Cup specialist or injury cover. Functional but limited ceiling.

**73-77 -- PL Survival Level / Lower-Table Starter.**
Starter for a relegation-battling side. Rotation depth for mid-table. The players that get promoted sides through the first season.
- ST benchmark: 0.10-0.14 G/90, 2-4 league goals. Limited quality but fills a role.
- CM benchmark: Workmanlike. Does a job defensively but limited progressive contribution.

**68-72 -- Championship-Level / PL Fringe.**
Below PL standard in most contexts. Starter in the Championship.

**60-67 -- Lower League / League One Level.**
Below PL viability. League One or League Two standard.

**Below 60 -- Non-Professional.**
Not sustainable at professional levels.
