# SOCCER SALARY & TRANSFER FEE FRAMEWORK -- v1

## Purpose
Maps KR tiers to transfer fee ranges and wage ranges across major leagues. Structured around what clubs are paying for at each KR level. Economic reference ranges reflect 2025-26 market realities.

## Key Difference From Basketball
Football has no salary cap (except emerging PSR/FFP constraints), no draft, and no standardized rookie scale. Transfer fees and wages are market-driven with massive variance by league, age, contract length, and selling club leverage. This framework provides ranges, not precise values.

## AGE-ADJUSTED TRANSFER FEE MODIFIERS

Transfer fees are heavily age-dependent. Same KR, different age = different fee.

| Age Band | Fee Modifier vs Base (24-26 peak) | Logic |
|----------|----------------------------------|-------|
| 16-18 | 0.3x-0.8x | Potential premium but limited production proof. Compensation-based for academy products. |
| 19-21 | 0.6x-1.2x | High upside. Resale value built in. Buyers pay for trajectory. |
| 22-23 | 0.8x-1.1x | Approaching peak. Strong evidence base. |
| 24-26 | 1.0x (reference) | Peak value. Maximum resale window closing. |
| 27-28 | 0.7x-0.9x | Still productive but resale value declining. |
| 29-30 | 0.4x-0.6x | Short-term value only. Depreciating asset. |
| 31-32 | 0.2x-0.4x | Bargain or free. Short contracts. |
| 33+ | Free-0.15x | Free transfers dominant. Wage is the cost, not the fee. |

### Contract Length Modifier
- 4+ years remaining: +15-25% above base (selling club leverage)
- 2-3 years remaining: base
- 1 year remaining: -30-50% (buyer leverage, free agent threat)
- 6 months or less: -50-80% or free (January window dynamic)
- Expired: Free transfer (solidarity/training compensation only for U23)

## SELL-ON CLAUSES & AGENT FEES

Standard market structures (2025-26):
- Agent fees: 5-15% of transfer fee (or equivalent in signing bonus / intermediary fee)
- Sell-on clauses: 10-25% of profit on next transfer (common for selling clubs)
- Buy-back clauses: EUR 20M-80M+ (used by elite clubs selling academy products)
- Loan fees: 5-15% of transfer value per season (plus wage contribution split)

## SALARY OUTPUT FORMAT

When providing transfer/wage intelligence for a player:

**Format:**
"[Player] at KR [X] in [League] projects to a transfer fee range of [range] and wages of [range] pw. Age [Y] applies a [modifier] to the base. Contract status ([Z] years remaining) [adjusts/does not adjust] leverage."

**Example:**
"A KR 91 striker aged 24 in the Premier League projects to a transfer fee of £45-70M and wages of £100-180K pw. Age 24 is at peak valuation. 3 years remaining on contract provides selling club moderate leverage."

## GOVERNANCE NOTE
- All values are market estimates based on 2025-26 transaction data.
- Transfer fees and wages inflate annually. Expect 5-10% year-over-year growth in top-5 leagues.
- This framework provides ranges for intelligence purposes. Actual fees depend on negotiation, competition, urgency, and relationships.
- KR does not determine market value directly. KR measures football ability. The market prices ability, age, marketability, contract status, and leverage together.
