Overrides
1. Purpose
Overrides exist to capture rare, real-world basketball realities that are not fully expressed by attributes, archetypes, KaNeXT badges, or system risks.
They exist to:
correct known model blind spots
acknowledge extreme scarcity or translation effects
prevent under- or over-valuation in edge cases
Overrides are exceptions, not features.
2. Authority and Limits
Overrides:
modify Final KR only
do not change attributes
do not change archetypes
do not change confidence
do not change system fit logic
Overrides are applied after:
Base KR
System Fit
KaNeXT badges
They are applied before:
Final KR lock
Downstream interpretation
3. Rarity and Discipline
Overrides are intentionally:
few in number
tightly defined
difficult to trigger
They are not tuning knobs.
They exist only where:
basketball reality consistently violates model expectations
the violation is structural, not anecdotal
4. College vs Pro Application
College Overrides
Positive only
Designed to reflect:
lineup distortion
scarcity at the college level
structural advantages not fully priced by attributes
Some overrides are college-only and explicitly expire before pro translation
Pro Overrides
Negative overrides always apply
Negative overrides supersede positives
Reflect:
translation failure
structural disqualification
professional viability constraints
At the pro reference level, realism is prioritized over upside.
5. Non-Stacking Rule
Overrides do not stack.
Rules:
Maximum one positive override
At pro reference:
negative overrides always apply
negative overrides override positives
This prevents narrative construction or KR manipulation.
6. Determinism
Override application is:
rule-based
deterministic
auditable
If conditions are met, the override applies.
If conditions are not met, it does not.
There is no discretion inside the engine.
7. Relationship to KaNeXT badges and System Risks
KaNeXT badges explain outperformance within the model
Overrides correct where the model structurally cannot
System Risks constrain interpretation after all correction
Overrides are the only mechanism allowed to directly adjust Final KR outside the base pipeline.
8. Authority and Change Control
This section is authoritative for:
override existence
trigger definitions
magnitude bounds
stacking rules
Any change requires:
explicit versioning
documented justification
empirical evidence
