Modifier Framework (Simulation Engine) — Authoritative
Purpose
The Modifier Framework governs how contextual pressure and interaction effects are applied inside the Simulation Engine.
Modifiers exist to:
bend likelihoods

shape distributions

express structural lean

Modifiers do not create events.
 They do not force outcomes.
Canonical Definition (LOCKED)
A modifier is a bounded adjustment applied to a probability distribution or rate inside the Possession Engine, prior to possession resolution.
Modifiers:
do not create actions

do not assign decisions

do not change ratings

do not override resolution logic
 They only adjust how likely outcomes are.

Canonical Role Within Simulation Engine (LOCKED)
The Modifier Framework is the last place contextual pressure may be introduced after governance and before possession resolution.

Lock rule: The Modifier Framework does not decide what applies — it only composes and applies what governance permits.
Authority and Non-Authority (LOCKED)
Authorized to:
adjust probability distributions (shot-type mix)

adjust efficiency rates (by shot type)

adjust turnover likelihood

adjust foul likelihood

adjust offensive rebound probability

adjust pace (system layer only)

Not authorized to:
force states

force actions

change initiators

override possession resolution

alter Player / Team / Development Intelligence outputs

Modifiers bend outcomes; they never decide them.
Valid Modifier Targets (LOCKED)
Only the following targets may be modified:
A) Shot-Type Distribution (must renormalize to 100%)
rim attempt probability

midrange attempt probability

three-point attempt probability

B) Shot Efficiency (conditional by shot type)
rim FG%

midrange FG%

three-point FG%

C) Turnover Probability
live-ball turnover likelihood

dead-ball turnover likelihood (optional V1, allowed)

D) Foul-Draw Probability
shooting foul probability

non-shooting foul probability

E) Offensive Rebound Probability
probability a missed shot results in an offensive rebound

Clarification (LOCKED): An offensive rebound ends the possession and initiates a new possession (scramble carryover rules defined in Possession Engine).
F) Pace Modifier (System-Level Only)
possessions-per-game multiplier
 Applied only in System × System interactions.

🚫 No other targets are permitted.
Modifier Types (LOCKED)
Type 1 — Additive (Δ)
Adds/subtracts a fixed delta to a probability.
 Examples:
+0.03 to three-point attempt probability

−0.02 to turnover probability

Use when:
shifting distribution mix

nudging rates (not scaling performance)

Type 2 — Multiplicative (×)
Scales a base probability or efficiency.
 Examples:
three-point efficiency × 1.08

turnover probability × 1.12

Use when:
modifying conversion quality

reflecting efficiency punishments/boosts

Global Bounds (Hard Guardrails) — LOCKED
A) Shot-Type Distribution Bounds
max shift per layer: ±6 percentage points

max total shift across all layers: ±10 percentage points

B) Efficiency Bounds
max multiplicative shift per layer: ±12%

max total efficiency shift (all layers combined): ±18%

C) Turnover & Foul Bounds
max per-layer shift: ±15%

max combined shift: ±22%

D) Pace Bounds (System Layer Only)
max pace multiplier: ×1.08

min pace multiplier: ×0.92

These bounds are non-negotiable and enforced before possession resolution.
Composition Rules (How Modifiers Stack) — LOCKED
Modifiers are applied in fixed order and composed, not overwritten.
Order of Application (LOCKED)
System × System modifiers

Archetype × System modifiers

Matchup-approved residuals (only if governance permits; no double-count)

Composition Logic (LOCKED)
additive modifiers are summed → then bounded

multiplicative modifiers are multiplied → then bounded

additive adjustments occur before multiplicative scaling

Conflict & Precedence Rules (LOCKED)
To prevent double-counting:
Coverage / matchup governance overrides archetype: if a variable is explained at a higher-authority layer, lower-authority modifier for that variable is ignored.

Residual application only: the last layer applies only what hasn’t already been accounted for.

No direction flips: later layers may attenuate earlier effects, but may not reverse sign.

State Awareness (Hard Constraint) — LOCKED
Modifiers may be state-conditional, but never state-creating.
Allowed:
“In Scramble state, rim efficiency × 1.05”

“In Advantage state, turnover probability −8%”

Forbidden:
forcing a state change

skipping state transitions

State creation belongs exclusively to the Possession Engine.
Data Source Discipline (LOCKED)
Modifiers:
may reference inferred labels (system, archetype, matchup tags)

may reference film-derived attributes (contest level, help presence) if provided upstream

may not compute those attributes

All inputs are assumed to be supplied upstream.
