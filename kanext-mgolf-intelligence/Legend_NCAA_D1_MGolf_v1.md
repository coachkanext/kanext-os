# NCAA DIVISION I -- MEN'S GOLF PLAYER KR LEGEND v1
## lambda = 1.000 (Power Conference reference)

Interpretation anchor: Power 5 conferences (SEC, ACC, Big 12, Big Ten, Pac-12). Deep rosters, national recruiting, sustained competition against top-100 Golfstat fields. College KR reflects current college role and impact only. No pro projection language. All ratings assume KLVN normalization and course difficulty adjustment.

Scoring averages are adjusted (Golfstat or equivalent course difficulty normalization). All benchmarks are MEN'S ONLY.

**98-100 -- National Player of the Year / Transcendent.**
Program-defining talent. Adjusted scoring average at or below par for the season. Jack Nicklaus Award winner or finalist. Multiple individual tournament wins against elite fields. Leads team in every meaningful statistical category. WAGR top-20. National Player of the Year contender at every major outlet. The rare player who would contend on a professional tour while still in college. Reserved for generational single-season performers.

**95-97 -- Elite All-American / Franchise Anchor.**
Team's unquestioned number-one player. Adjusted scoring average under +1.0. First Team All-American. Conference Player of the Year contender. Multiple top-5 finishes against ranked fields. WAGR top-75. Counting score in virtually every round. The player the entire lineup is built around. NCAA Championship individual contender.

**92-94 -- High-Impact Starter / Core Winner.**
Wins rounds and tournaments at the highest level. Adjusted scoring average +0.5 to +1.5. All-American (first or second team). All-Conference. Counting score 90%+ of rounds. The player who delivers under pressure at NCAA Regionals and Championships. Top-3 on team in scoring. Trusted to anchor the lineup.

**89-91 -- Solid Starter / Reliable Counter.**
Firmly positive contributor at D1 Power level. Adjusted scoring average +1.5 to +2.5. All-Conference honorable mention range. Counting score 75-85% of rounds. Consistent mid-lineup player on nationally ranked teams. Rarely the dropped score. Conference tournament contributor.

**86-88 -- Lineup Regular / Counting Contributor.**
Earns a top-5 spot through consistency. Adjusted scoring average +2.5 to +3.5. Counting score 60-75% of rounds. The 3rd-4th player on competitive teams. May not carry the team but never hurts it. Steady enough to be relied on at conference and regional tournaments.

**83-85 -- Fringe Lineup / 5th Man.**
Competes for the 5th tournament spot. Adjusted scoring average +3.5 to +4.5. Counting score 40-60% of rounds. Provides insurance depth. Some tournaments in the lineup, some not. The player who needs to outperform in qualifying rounds to earn a spot.

**80-82 -- Roster Depth / Practice Player.**
On the competitive roster but rarely in the tournament lineup. Adjusted scoring average +4.5 to +6.0. Plays in fall events and lower-tier spring invitationals. Development candidate with upside if a specific component improves.

**77-79 -- Limited Roster / Non-Competitive.**
Rostered but not in the competitive plan. Adjusted scoring average +6.0 to +8.0. Practice rounds only. Walk-on or partial-scholarship depth.

**74-76 -- Developmental / Project.**
Future-oriented roster slot. Not currently viable in D1 Power competition. Needs significant development across multiple components.

**71-73 -- Below D1 Power Viability.**
Below competitive threshold at this level. Would be better served competing at a lower division.

**Below 71 -- Well Below D1 Power Viability.**
Significantly below the competitive threshold.
