# NCAA WOMEN'S FLAG FOOTBALL PLAYER KR LEGEND v1
## lambda = 1.000 (Reference - Projected)

PROVISIONAL LEGEND. NCAA women's flag football was added to the Emerging Sports for Women program in January 2026. No NCAA championship season has been completed. This legend is PROJECTED based on anticipated talent concentration when Power conference schools begin sponsoring programs. It will be recalibrated after the first full competitive seasons.

Interpretation anchor: Projected NCAA D1 programs at established schools (Alabama State, UT Arlington, Mercyhurst, Mount St. Mary's, Long Island, Cal Poly, Nebraska, and other P4/P5 schools adding programs by 2027-2028). Full scholarship equivalency support. National recruiting pipeline from 39 states with sanctioned high school flag football. NFL FLAG pipeline feeding top athletes. Multi-sport athletes (track, soccer, basketball) recruited for speed and agility.

**90-100 - National Player of the Year / Generational Talent.**
Transcendent athlete who dominates the NCAA flag football landscape. Elite production across every phase. Carries team to national championship contention. Consensus All-American. Olympic team candidate. The kind of player who elevates the sport's visibility nationally. Production profile: QB with 75%+ comp, 4:1+ TD:INT, 500+ rush yards AND 2500+ pass yards. OR WR with 80+ receptions, 15+ TDs, 16+ YPR. OR defensive player with 10+ INTs and 90%+ flag pull rate.

**86-89 - Franchise Anchor / Elite All-American.**
Clear number-one player on a nationally ranked team. Defines the program's identity. Conference POY. First-team All-American. 20+ total TDs or equivalent defensive dominance. The player the entire game plan revolves around.

**82-85 - High-Impact Starter / All-Conference Lock.**
Primary reason strong teams win games. Heavy usage. Consistent two-way production if applicable. All-Conference first or second team. Game-changer in conference play. Drives deep postseason runs.

**78-81 - Solid Starter / Quality Contributor.**
Reliable starter on a competitive team. Consistent production. Trusted in critical situations. All-Conference caliber. Clear role and consistent execution within it.

**74-77 - Starter / Role Player.**
Earns a starting spot through effort and competence. Contributes within a defined role. Not the go-to player but a solid piece of the roster. May excel on one side of the ball.

**70-73 - Rotation Player / Specialist.**
Top-10 on the roster. Contributes in specific situations (red zone, 4th-down defense, specific formation packages). 40-60% of snaps on one or both sides.

**66-69 - Depth / Developmental.**
On the active roster. Gets occasional game snaps. Primary value is in practice and future development. 1-2 years from contributing role.

**62-65 - Roster / Limited Contribution.**
Makes the roster but rarely sees competitive snaps. Practice player with future potential.

**58-61 - Walk-On / Fringe Roster.**
On the team through effort. Not in the competitive rotation. Development project or roster filler.

**Below 58 - Below NCAA Flag Football Viability.**
Below the competitive threshold for NCAA women's flag football.

## CALIBRATION NOTE

This legend is entirely PROJECTED. No NCAA flag football season has been completed at the time of writing (March 2026). The tier boundaries, production profiles, and labels are estimated based on:
1. NAIA flag football data (2021-present) scaled by KLVN lambda
2. Projected talent concentration at NCAA D1 based on school announcements and recruiting pipelines
3. Comparison to other NCAA emerging sports in their early years
4. NFL FLAG and USA Football athlete development data

Expect significant recalibration after the first 2-3 NCAA competitive seasons. Tier boundaries may shift by 3-5 KR points in either direction.
