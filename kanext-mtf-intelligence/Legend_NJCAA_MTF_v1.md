# NJCAA - MEN'S TRACK AND FIELD PLAYER KR LEGEND v1

## Interpretation Anchor
NJCAA men's track and field (covers D1, D2, D3). NJCAA D1 is the highest JUCO level with full scholarships. Athletes are typically 2-year competitors before transferring to 4-year programs. Top NJCAA D1 athletes can be elite - some are D1 transfers or international athletes. KR reflects current ability regardless of future transfer destination. All KR ratings assume verified marks (FAT timing, wind-legal for applicable events).
