## Reading Guide

**If you have 15 minutes:** Read the Executive Summary (1.1) only. It contains the complete thesis.

**If you have 1 hour:** Read Start Here (1.1-1.3) + The Deal (2.1-2.4). You will understand what KaNeXT is, what the investment terms are, where the money goes, and how it is protected.

**If you have 3 hours:** Add The Money (Section 3) and The Institution (Section 4). You will understand the revenue model, the cost structure, and how SFBC specifically works under the partnership.

**If you are doing full diligence:** Read everything. Start with Sections 1-4 in order, then go to whichever section addresses your primary area of concern (product, growth, acquisitions, regulatory, risk). The documents are designed to be read independently, but they reference each other where relevant.

**If you have one specific question:** Use the table above to find the document that answers it. Every document has a Purpose section at the top explaining what question it addresses.
