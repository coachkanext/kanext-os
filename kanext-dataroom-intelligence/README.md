## About The Partner Institution

Throughout most of this data room, you'll see references to "the partner institution" or "the Year 1 partnership" rather than a specific institution name. This is deliberate. KaNeXT is an institutional operating system designed to deploy at qualifying partner institutions. The Year 1 deployment is at one specific institution, but the architecture is portable.

The specific Year 1 partner institution is South Florida Bible College (SFBC), a forty-year-old ABHE-accredited evangelical Christian institution in Deerfield Beach, Florida. SFBC is named where the institutional identity matters - primarily in sections 02 (The Deal), 04 (The Institution), and 08 (Regulatory and Compliance). In other sections, where the architecture, product, growth strategy, or intelligence is discussed, "the partner institution" is used to reflect that the system is partner-agnostic.
