3) Participation% (Rotation Weights) Built
Input: minutes played (official or coach-entered)

Convert to Participation% (on-court share)

Include players with Participation% ≥ 5%

Renormalize included players so Σ Participation% = 100%

Display as % (ex: 22%), store internally as 0.22


4) Team Offense KR (Aggregation)
Team_Off_KR = Σ( Player_Final_System_Off_KRᵢ × Participation%ᵢ )

5) Team Defense KR (Aggregation)
Team_Def_KR = Σ( Player_Final_System_Def_KRᵢ × Participation%ᵢ )

6) Overall Team KR (Locked 53/47)
Overall_Team_KR = (Team_Off_KR × 0.53) + (Team_Def_KR × 0.47)
