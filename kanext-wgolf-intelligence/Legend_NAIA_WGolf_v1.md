## NAIA EVALUATION CONTEXT

**NAIA women's golf has 5 scholarships.** This is fewer than D1 (6) and D2 (5.4) but still allows meaningful support for top lineup players.

**NAIA golf talent is concentrated in specific programs.** Dalton State, SCAD Savannah, Oklahoma City, Keiser, and a few others consistently produce nationally competitive teams. Most NAIA programs are smaller and less funded.

**NAIA national championship is a key evaluation event.** The NAIA National Championship provides the best data for evaluating top NAIA players against a quality field.

**Transfer from NAIA to D1/D2 is increasingly common.** Players who dominate at NAIA (scoring average under 74.5) are strong candidates to transfer up.

**International players at NAIA.** Some NAIA programs actively recruit international players who may have strong amateur backgrounds but need development before competing at D1.
