Many NAIA programs compete in both sideline and competition formats. Talent at the top can rival mid-tier NCAA programs but depth drops off faster. Programs like those in the Heart, SSAC, GPAC, and similar NAIA conferences. Some NAIA programs are among the early adopters of STUNT format.
