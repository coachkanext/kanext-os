Defensive Archetype × Offensive System
Purpose
This section governs how defensive player archetypes interact with opposing offensive systems at the possession level.
It answers:
How does this type of defender express pressure, disruption, or vulnerability inside a given offensive structure?
It does not evaluate players.
It does not evaluate systems.
Scope
This section operates at the defender–system interaction level.
It governs:
shot profile pressure
efficiency suppression or leakage
turnover pressure
foul and free-throw impact
pace and flow modifiers (where applicable)
It does not:
modify Player KRs
modify archetype classification
change usage or role
imply defensive value or ranking
provide coaching advice
All outputs are interaction deltas only.

apply only inside Projection
never write back upstream
Volatility may increase, but mean values remain governed.

Defensive Archetype × Offensive Systems
Point-of-Attack Stopper (POA Stopper) — vs all 11 Offensive Systems
Output format (locked): explicit PP deltas applied inside  possession translation.
Key (what “PP” means here): percentage-point shifts to possession outcome rates / shot diet shares.
ΔTOV% = turnover rate change (pp of possessions)
ΔFT% = FT possession rate change (pp of possessions)
Δ3AR / ΔRimAR / ΔMidAR = shot-attempt share shifts (pp of FGA distribution)
ΔPace = possessions per 100 shift (pp100)
POA Stopper identity assumption (governed): elite containment at the point, screen navigation, reduces paint touches and clean pull-ups; increases “late-clock” possessions.
1) Spread Pick-and-Roll (Off) vs POA Stopper
ΔTOV%: +1.5pp
ΔFT%: -0.6pp
Δ3AR: -1.5pp (specifically pull-up 3 quality suppressed)
ΔRimAR: -2.0pp
ΔMidAR: +3.5pp (more late-clock/floaters/mid pull-ups)
ΔPace: -0.8 pp100
Why: POA kills initial advantage → fewer downhill rim attempts, more stalled midrange.
2) 5-Out Motion (Off) vs POA Stopper
ΔTOV%: +0.8pp
ΔFT%: -0.4pp
Δ3AR: -0.8pp
ΔRimAR: -1.0pp
ΔMidAR: +1.8pp
ΔPace: -0.4 pp100
Why: 5-out can re-flow, but POA reduces clean blow-bys that power drive-kick.
