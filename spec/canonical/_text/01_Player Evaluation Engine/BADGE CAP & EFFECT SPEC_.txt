BADGE CAP & EFFECT SPEC — COLLEGE v1
Purpose
Define how much conditional skill expression (KaNeXT badges) is allowed to influence player ratings without overriding Base KR truth, Archetype identity, or System Fit.
KaNeXT badges amplify certified skills; they do not create quality.
KaNeXT badges only exist when a skill translates at the high-major level.
1. Badge Eligibility Prerequisite (Hard Gate)
Before any badge can exist, both of the following must be true:
Relevant Trait(s) ≥ 90
Corresponding Component KR ≥ 90
Silver and Gold KaNeXT badges require thresholds of 94 and 97 respectively for both trait(s) and component KR.
If this prerequisite is not met → the badge does not exist.
KaNeXT badges are certifications, not projections.
2. Badge Levels & Component Effects
Component scale: 0–100
Badge effects apply only to the relevant component(s).

3. Global Badge Caps (Hard Limits)
Caps exist as safety rails, not balance crutches.
They are expected to bind rarely, only in extreme stacked cases.
3.1 Badge Count Caps
Max Gold KaNeXT badges: 1
Max Silver KaNeXT badges: 3
Bronze KaNeXT badges: remainder (no explicit count cap)
This prevents multi-weapon inflation while still allowing narrow specialists.
3.2 Per-Component Cap
Maximum badge impact per single component: ≤ +12
Examples:
1 Gold (+10) + 1 Bronze (+3) → capped at +12
2 Silvers (+6 + +6) → capped at +12
No single skill domain may be rewritten by KaNeXT badges alone.
3.3 Overall Impact Cap
Total badge contribution to Final KR: ≤ +3.5
This ensures:
KaNeXT badges can matter
KaNeXT badges can decide margins
KaNeXT badges cannot manufacture tier jumps
Base KR + System Fit remain the primary truth
4. Stacking Rules (Linear, v1)
Badge effects are additive and linear
No diminishing returns in v1
No probabilistic scaling
When a cap is reached, excess effect is truncated (never redistributed)
This guarantees behavior that is:
Deterministic
Transparent
Auditable

5. Guardrails (Non-Negotiable)
KaNeXT badges DO NOT:
❌ Change Base KR
❌ Change Archetype or archetype confidence
❌ Override archetype misfits
❌ Bypass System Fit weighting
❌ Exceed component or overall caps
Overrides remain the only mechanism permitted to correct model misinterpretation.
6. Design Intent (Why This Is Correct)
Bronze = certified high-major competence
Silver = real, reliable schematic advantage
Gold = true college-level weapon
A Gold badge will often move a player ~+1 to +2 Overall KR in favorable systems:
Enough to matter
Never enough to lie
KaNeXT badges explain outperformance, they never replace evaluation.
