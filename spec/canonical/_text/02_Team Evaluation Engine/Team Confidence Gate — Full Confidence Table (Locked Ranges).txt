Team Confidence Gate (Percentage) — Locked Doc
Purpose
Confidence% is a single number that communicates evidence completeness + stability for the team evaluation.
Output
team_kr_confidence_pct ∈ [0,100]
coverage_confidence_pct ∈ [0,100]
Computed at the end of team evaluation.
What it affects
Confidence% does not change any Team KR math.
Confidence% is used for transparency and (optionally) gating what the system is allowed to claim or trigger.
