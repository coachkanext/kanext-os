# Legend - NJCAA Women's Track and Field v1

---

## SCOPE
KR tier interpretation for NJCAA women's track and field.
All benchmarks women's-specific.
NJCAA has three divisions. D1 offers up to 20 athletic scholarships (tuition, fees, room, board, books, transportation). D2 offers tuition and fees only. D3 offers no athletic scholarships.
This legend covers all three divisions with separate tier tables.

- Hurdle heights: 100mH 0.838m/33", 400mH 0.762m/30".
- Multi-event is Heptathlon (not Decathlon).
- NJCAA D1/D2/D3 are separate competitive tiers with separate benchmarks.
- Cross-level comparison requires KLVN lambda normalization.
- All KR tiers display-only. The legend does not produce KR; it interprets it.
- v1 benchmarks estimated from NJCAA championship results and transfer production data.
