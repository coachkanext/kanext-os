# KaNeXT LLC - Ownership and Governance Summary

## OWNERSHIP STRUCTURE

This structure is designed to preserve clear founder ownership, segregate intellectual property from operations, and distinguish fund LP interests from operating-company equity.

Oluwadara Samuel Kalejaiye (Sammy Kalejaiye) owns 100% of OSK Group LLC, a Florida holding company. OSK Group LLC owns 100% of all operating subsidiaries:

- **KaNeXT LLC** (FL) - Operating company. General Partner of KaNeXT Capital Fund I LLC.
- **KaNeXT IP LLC** (FL) - Holds all intellectual property, including the intelligence system, KaNeXT OS architecture, all canonical specifications, and trademarks. KaNeXT LLC operates under license from KaNeXT IP LLC.
- **KaNeXT Capital Fund I LLC** (FL) - Fund entity. GP is KaNeXT LLC.

No equity in any entity has been issued or transferred to any party other than the founder as of the date of this document.

---

## FUND INVESTORS - OWNERSHIP POSITION

Investors in KaNeXT Capital Fund I LLC hold LP interests in the fund entity only. Fund investors own 0% of OSK Group LLC, KaNeXT LLC, KaNeXT IP LLC, or any affiliated entity. LP interests do not convert to equity in any operating or holding entity under any circumstances.

Fund investors have no board seats, no voting rights, no governance rights, and no conversion mechanism with respect to any entity in the corporate structure.

---

## GOVERNANCE

All entities are governed solely by their equity holders. As of the date of this document, that is Oluwadara Samuel Kalejaiye exclusively, through OSK Group LLC.

No outside board seats exist. No advisory board holds governance authority. No investor, whether through a fund, infrastructure vehicle, or otherwise, holds any right to appoint directors, approve decisions, veto actions, or participate in operational or strategic decision-making.

This reflects the current governance structure as of the date of this document and does not preclude future board formation, equity grants, or governance changes at the founder's discretion.
