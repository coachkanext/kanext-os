Interpretation anchor: Power conferences + top programs (SEC, Big 12, ACC, Pac-12 successor, Big Ten, plus historically elite programs like Oklahoma, UCLA, Alabama, Florida regardless of realignment). Deep rosters, pro-pipeline recruiting, sustained play vs RPI Top-50 opponents. 56-game regular season standard. College KR reflects current college role and impact only. No pro language, no pro projection. All ratings assume KLVN normalization.

DUAL TRACK: Every tier describes BOTH hitter and pitcher profiles. A player is evaluated on the appropriate track based on primary role. Slap hitters are evaluated on the hitter track with slap-specific production profiles noted.
