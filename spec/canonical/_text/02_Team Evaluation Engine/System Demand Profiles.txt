Offensive System Demand Profiles (11)
1) Spread Pick-and-Roll
Critical archetypes (A): Pick-and-Roll Operator; Vertical Spacer (Rim Runner); Stretch Big (Pick-and-Pop); Spot-Up Specialist
High (B): 3-and-D Wing; Two-Way Wing; Connector Guard / Wing
Optional (C): Secondary Creator Wing; Short-Roll Playmaker Big
Ideal modifiers:
Primary Engine (on the Pick-and-Roll Operator)

Force Multiplier (on at least 1 shooter wing)

Specialist Anchor (often on rim protector / glass guy if present)

 Critical-missing risk: no Primary Engine handler, or no roll/pop gravity big → offense collapses into low-quality pull-ups.


2) 5-Out Motion
A: Connector Guard / Wing; Off-Ball Shooter (Movement); Stretch Big (Pick-and-Pop) or Small-Ball Big (Switch 5); Slasher / Rim Pressure Wing
B: Two-Way Wing; Spot-Up Specialist
C: Secondary Creator Wing; DHO / Handoff Hub
Ideal modifiers:
Force Multiplier (2+ guys)

Secondary Engine (1 guy max—closeout punisher)

 Critical-missing risk: no connector + no movement shooter → motion becomes empty passing with no bend.


3) Motion / Read & React
A: Connector Guard / Wing; Off-Ball Shooter (Movement); Slasher / Rim Pressure Wing; DHO / Handoff Hub
B: Two-Way Wing; Secondary Creator Wing; Short-Roll Playmaker Big
C: Spot-Up Specialist; Stretch Big
Ideal modifiers:
Force Multiplier (connectors/shooters)

Secondary Engine (1 creator)

 Critical-missing risk: lack of decision-speed connector(s) → turnovers, stalled flow, no advantage chain.


4) Pace & Space
A: Primary Ball-Handler (Offense-First) or Pick-and-Roll Operator; Vertical Spacer; Spot-Up Specialist
B: Slasher / Rim Pressure Wing; 3-and-D Wing; Connector Guard / Wing
C: Stretch Big; Secondary Creator Wing
Ideal modifiers:
Primary Engine (lead handler)

Force Multiplier (spacing wings)

 Critical-missing risk: no rim pressure (slasher or rim runner) → you get “air spacing” with no paint collapse.


5) Dribble Drive
A: Slasher / Rim Pressure Wing; Primary Ball-Handler (Offense-First); Spot-Up Specialist (2+)
B: Secondary Creator Wing; Vertical Spacer; Two-Way Wing
C: Stretch Big; Connector Guard / Wing
Ideal modifiers:
Primary Engine (ball pressure breaker)

Force Multiplier (elite spacer / gravity)

 Critical-missing risk: no high-end slasher + no spacing → drive lanes die, turnovers spike.


6) Princeton
A: Post Hub / Facilitator Big; Connector Guard / Wing; Off-Ball Shooter (Movement)
B: Two-Way Wing; Slasher / Rim Pressure Wing
C: Spot-Up Specialist; Secondary Creator Wing
Ideal modifiers:
Force Multiplier (post hub passer + cutters/shooters)

Secondary Engine optional (late-clock bailout)

 Critical-missing risk: no true post hub → you’re just “passing around the perimeter” with no trigger.


7) Flex
A: Post Scorer (Back-to-Basket) or Post Hub / Facilitator Big; Off-Ball Shooter (Movement); Spot-Up Specialist
B: Two-Way Wing; Connector Guard / Wing
C: Slasher / Rim Pressure Wing
Ideal modifiers:
Specialist Anchor (elite post scorer) or Force Multiplier (post hub)

 Critical-missing risk: if the post isn’t a real threat or hub, flex actions don’t punish and become predictable.


8) Swing
A: Connector Guard / Wing; Spot-Up Specialist (2+); Secondary Creator Wing
B: Two-Way Wing; Stretch Big
C: Slasher / Rim Pressure Wing; DHO / Handoff Hub
Ideal modifiers:
Force Multiplier (spacing + low-usage efficiency)

Secondary Engine (one shot-maker)

 Critical-missing risk: no secondary creator → you reverse forever but can’t break set defenses.


9) Post-Centric / Inside-Out
A: Post Scorer (Back-to-Basket) or Post Hub / Facilitator Big; Spot-Up Specialist (2+); 3-and-D Wing
B: Slasher / Rim Pressure Wing; Rebounding / Interior Enforcer
C: Stretch Big; Secondary Creator Wing
Ideal modifiers:
Specialist Anchor (post scorer) or Force Multiplier (post hub)

Force Multiplier (elite shooters)

 Critical-missing risk: no shooting around post → doubles win, post touches become turnovers.


10) Moreyball
A: Pick-and-Roll Operator; Vertical Spacer; Spot-Up Specialist (2+); 3-and-D Wing
B: Stretch Big; Slasher / Rim Pressure Wing
C: Secondary Creator Wing
Ideal modifiers:
Primary Engine (PnR)

Force Multiplier (shooting gravity)

 Critical-missing risk: if you can’t generate rim/3 volume (via engine + spacing) the math advantage disappears.


11) Heliocentric
A: Primary Ball-Handler (Offense-First); Spot-Up Specialist (2+); 3-and-D Wing
B: Vertical Spacer or Stretch Big; Secondary Creator Wing (optional)
C: Connector Guard / Wing
Ideal modifiers:
Primary Engine (heliocentric star — non-negotiable)

Force Multiplier (2+ spacers/defenders)

 Critical-missing risk: no true Primary Engine = system can’t exist; also if spacers aren’t real, the star gets swarmed.


Defensive System Demand Profiles (9)
1) Containment Man
A: POA Defender Guard; Rim Protector Anchor; Switchable Defender Wing
B: Two-Way Wing; Rebounding / Interior Enforcer
C: Small-Ball Big (Switch 5)
Ideal modifiers: Specialist Anchor (rim protector or POA stopper), Force Multiplier (disruptor/cleaner)
Critical-missing risk: no rim protector anchor → blow-bys become layup lines.

2) Pack Line
A: Rim Protector Anchor; Rebounding / Interior Enforcer; Two-Way Wing
B: POA Defender Guard; 3-and-D Wing
C: Switchable Defender Wing
Ideal modifiers: Specialist Anchor (paint anchor), Force Multiplier (rebound + low-foul defenders)
Critical-missing risk: if your anchor can’t protect without fouling → pack line collapses.

3) Pressure Man (Denial)
A: POA Defender Guard; Switchable Defender Wing; Energy Bench Spark
B: Two-Way Wing; Rim Protector Anchor
C: Small-Ball Big (Switch 5)
Ideal modifiers: Force Multiplier (pressure/chaos), Specialist Anchor (matchup stopper)
Critical-missing risk: pressure with no backline eraser = free rim.

4) Switch Everything
A: Switchable Defender Wing (multiple); Small-Ball Big (Switch 5); POA Defender Guard
B: Two-Way Wing
C: Rim Protector Anchor (optional depending on personnel)
Ideal modifiers: Force Multiplier (switchable stoppers), Specialist Anchor (elite 1–4 stopper)
Critical-missing risk: if your 4/5 can’t survive switches, the whole identity breaks.

5) ICE / No-Middle
A: POA Defender Guard; Rim Protector Anchor; Two-Way Wing
B: 3-and-D Wing; Rebounding / Interior Enforcer
C: Switchable Defender Wing
Ideal modifiers: Specialist Anchor (rim protector), Force Multiplier (clean rotations + contest)
Critical-missing risk: if POA can’t angle + contain, the scheme gets split repeatedly.

6) Zone (Structured)
A: Rim Protector Anchor; Rebounding / Interior Enforcer; Two-Way Wing
B: 3-and-D Wing; POA Defender Guard (for top pressure)
C: Energy Bench Spark
Ideal modifiers: Specialist Anchor (rim + glass), Force Multiplier (rebound leverage / deflections)
Critical-missing risk: zone without rebounding dominance = you lose by extra possessions.

7) Matchup Zone / Hybrid
A: Switchable Defender Wing; Two-Way Wing; Rim Protector Anchor
B: POA Defender Guard; Energy Bench Spark
C: Small-Ball Big (Switch 5)
Ideal modifiers: Force Multiplier (versatility), Specialist Anchor (stopper role)
Critical-missing risk: if wings can’t guard in space, hybrid turns into scramble.

8) Press / Pressure Defense
A: Energy Bench Spark; POA Defender Guard; Switchable Defender Wing
B: Rim Protector Anchor; Two-Way Wing
C: Rebounding / Interior Enforcer
Ideal modifiers: Force Multiplier (defensive playmaking), Specialist Anchor (backline eraser)
Critical-missing risk: no rim protection behind press → it’s a layup drill.

9) Junk / Special
A: Specialist archetype matching the junk (usually POA Defender Guard / Switchable Defender Wing) + Rim Protector Anchor
B: Two-Way Wing; 3-and-D Wing
C: Energy Bench Spark
Ideal modifiers: Specialist Anchor (narrow elite job), Force Multiplier (support glue)
Critical-missing risk: if you don’t have a true matchup stopper, junk doesn’t “steal possessions.”
