# NJCAA -- MEN'S GOLF PLAYER KR LEGEND v1
## lambda = 0.700 (D1 reference), 0.620 (D2), 0.540 (D3)

Interpretation anchor: NJCAA (junior college / community college) programs. Two-year institutions with high roster turnover. Scholarship support available at D1 (8 full) and D2 (8 full) levels. D3 has no athletic scholarships. NJCAA serves as a critical development pathway - many golfers use JUCO to develop before transferring to 4-year programs. College KR reflects current college role and impact only. No pro projection. All ratings assume KLVN normalization and course difficulty adjustment.

Scoring averages are adjusted for course difficulty. All benchmarks are MEN'S ONLY.

---

## NJCAA D1 (lambda = 0.700)

**94-100 -- Transcendent / National Player of the Year.**
Historically elite at NJCAA D1. Adjusted scoring average at or below par. NJCAA National Championship individual contender or winner. First Team All-American. Projects as high-impact player at D2 or counting player at D1 mid-major after transfer. The rare JUCO talent with D1 Power upside.

**90-93 -- Elite All-American / Program Anchor.**
Team's top player. Adjusted scoring average under +2.0. All-American. Conference/Region Player of the Year contender. Counting score every round. Multiple individual wins. NJCAA National Championship qualifier. Strong transfer candidate to D1 or high D2.

**86-89 -- High-Impact Starter / All-Conference.**
Consistent producer at NJCAA D1 level. Adjusted scoring average +2.0 to +4.0. All-Conference/Region selection. Counting score 85%+ of rounds. Top-3 on team. Projects as counting player at D2 or lineup contributor at D1 low-major after transfer.

**83-85 -- Solid Starter / Reliable Counter.**
Consistent counting player. Adjusted scoring average +4.0 to +6.0. Counting score 70-80% of rounds. Solid D2 or NAIA transfer candidate.

**80-82 -- Lineup Regular / Contributor.**
Earns top-5 spot. Adjusted scoring average +6.0 to +8.0. Counting score 55-70% of rounds. D2/NAIA/D3 transfer candidate.

**77-79 -- Fringe Lineup / 5th Man.**
Competes for final lineup spot. Adjusted scoring average +8.0 to +10.0. Sometimes counts, sometimes dropped.

**74-76 -- Roster Depth.**
On the roster but rarely in tournament lineup. Development focus.

**71-73 -- Limited Roster / Developmental.**
Below competitive rotation at NJCAA D1. Practice depth.

**Below 71 -- Below NJCAA D1 Viability.**
Below competitive threshold at this level.

## NJCAA Transfer Projection Framework

NJCAA golfers are evaluated with an eye toward their transfer destination. The Level Tier Map is especially important for JUCO players because it shows coaches at 4-year programs what role the player projects at their level.

Key NJCAA-to-4-year transfer benchmarks:
- KR 90+: D1 Power counting player projection
- KR 86-89: D1 mid-major counting player or D1 Power fringe lineup
- KR 82-85: D2 high-impact or D1 low-major lineup contributor
- KR 78-81: D2 counting player or NAIA high-impact
- KR 74-77: NAIA counting player or D3 high-impact
- Below 74: D3 or NAIA roster depth
