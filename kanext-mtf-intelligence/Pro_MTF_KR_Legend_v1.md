# PRO MEN'S TRACK AND FIELD - KR LEGEND v1

## Interpretation Anchor
Professional men's track and field. This legend covers the global professional landscape from Diamond League/World Championships/Olympics down through Continental Tour levels and post-collegiate competition. KR is universal - the same number that rates a college athlete rates a professional. This legend interprets what that number means in the professional context.

## PRO LANDSCAPE NOTES

### Major Professional Competition Tiers

**Tier 1: Diamond League and Championships**
- World Athletics Diamond League (14 meetings + Final)
- Olympic Games (every 4 years)
- World Athletics Championships (biennial)
- World Athletics Indoor Championships (biennial)
- World Athletics Relays

**Tier 2: Continental Tour and National Championships**
- Continental Tour Gold (8-10 meetings)
- Continental Tour Silver
- Continental Tour Bronze
- US Olympic Trials / US Championships
- European Athletics Championships
- Commonwealth Games
- Major national championships (UK, Germany, France, Kenya, Ethiopia, Jamaica)

**Tier 3: Regional and Domestic Pro Meets**
- Regional track meets and invitational series
- USATF Grand Prix events
- European permit meetings
- Asian Athletics Championships
- African Athletics Championships

### World Athletics World Rankings

World Athletics maintains official world rankings that determine entry into competitions:
- Rankings are based on competition results weighted by meet quality and placement
- Target rankings thresholds are used for World Championship and Olympic qualification
- Rankings serve as a backup qualification pathway when entry standards are not met
