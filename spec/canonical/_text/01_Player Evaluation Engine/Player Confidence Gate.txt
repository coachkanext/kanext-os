Confidence Gate (Percentage) — Locked Doc
Purpose
Confidence% is a single number that communicates evidence completeness + stability for the evaluation.
Output
confidence_pct ∈ [0,100]
Computed at the end of player evaluation.
What it affects
Confidence% does not change any KR math.
Confidence% is used for transparency and (optionally) gating what the system is allowed to claim or trigger.
